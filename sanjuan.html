Explore San Juan, Puerto Rico

Find the perfect destination based on your travel style.

San Juan, Puerto Rico is the vibrant capital and cultural heart of Puerto Rico. Founded in 1521, it is one of the oldest European-established cities in the Americas. San Juan blends historic Spanish colonial architecture with modern Caribbean energy. The city is famous for Old San Juan, with its colorful buildings, cobblestone streets, and historic forts like Castillo San Felipe del Morro. Visitors enjoy beautiful beaches, lively music, rich cuisine, and a unique mix of Spanish, African, and Taíno cultural influences. San Juan offers a perfect combination of history, relaxation, nightlife, and tropical beauty — making it one of the most popular destinations in the Caribbean.
