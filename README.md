# Explore America

Explore America is a travel web app we built for CS 430 with Professor Bryan Harley. The idea was simple — help people figure out where to travel based on their vibe. You pick between a cold urban city or a warm tropical destination and the site takes you there with photos, videos, and a full trip planner.

Live site: [https://waril2001.github.io/Explore-america-multimedia/](https://waril2001.github.io/Explore-america-multimedia/)

## What the site does

- You land on the home page and pick Cold & Urban or Warm & Tropical
- Seattle page has a video, photo gallery, fun facts, and ambient audio
- San Juan page has a full beach background, animated icon, and top attractions
- The planner lets you build a day-by-day itinerary based on your travel style, trip length, and budget
- There is also an About page, Contact form, and a Credits page with all media sources cited

## How to run it locally

1. Clone the repo
2. Open index.html in your browser

No installs needed, it is all vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS.
